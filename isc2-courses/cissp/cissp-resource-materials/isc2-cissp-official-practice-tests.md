# CISSP Official Practice Tests

All participants to our ISC2 CISSP trainings get a free copy of the ISC2 CISSP Official Practice Tests.

CISSP Official ISC2 Practice Tests provides you with 1300 unique practice questions, covering all CISSP exam domains. These practice tests align with the 2018 version of the exam to ensure up-to-date preparation, and are designed to cover what you'll see on exam day.
