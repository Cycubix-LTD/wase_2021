# CCSP Official Practice Tests

All participants to our ISC2 CCSP trainings get a free copy of the ISC2 CCSP Official Practice Tests.

With over 1,000 practice questions, this book gives you the opportunity to test your level of understanding and gauge your readiness for the CCSP exam long before the big day. These questions cover 100% of the CCSP exam domains and include answers with full explanations to help you understand the reasoning and approach for each. Logical organization by domain allows you to practice only the areas you need to bring you up to par, without wasting precious time on topics you've already mastered.
