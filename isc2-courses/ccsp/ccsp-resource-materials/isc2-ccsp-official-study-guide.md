# CCSP Official Study Guide

All participants to our ISC2 CCSP trainings get a free copy of the ISC2 CCSP Official Study Guide.

The ISC2 Certified Cloud Security Professional Official Study Guide covers 100% of the CCSP Common Body of Knowledge (CBK) and includes expert content, real-world practice, access to the Sybex online interactive learning environment and much more.
